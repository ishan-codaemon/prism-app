Replace a non-working or faulty sensor

Each CGM manufacturer has a replacement process to help improve their products.

Follow these steps to get a replacement • Do NOT throw away your damaged or non-working sensor • Freestyle Libre CGM users, contact Abbott Customer service at 855-632-8658, or CLICK HERE for Abbott's online replacement form. • Dexcom G7 CGM users, contact Dexcom Customer service at 888-738-3646, or CLICK HERE for Dexcom's online replacement form. • Ask for expedited delivery and REFUSE a pharmacy coupon as it will require a new RX from your doctor.
